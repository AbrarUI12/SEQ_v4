## 5. Interpretation

- **PPL-degrade ground truth is under-powered.** Up to 40% of modules show *negative* ΔPPL and up to 74% sit below the noise floor — only a `down_proj` and `lm_head` rise above it. Rankings on it are mostly noise (entropy leads at ρ=0.16 but that is a weak-field artifact).

## 6. Recommended next experiment

1. **Switch to the reconstruction ground truth** (`--ground_truth recon`) to remove PPL noise and reach per-channel granularity.
2. **Normalize the extensive signals** (`_pp` variants) for a fair cross-size ranking.
3. **Downstream Pareto**: allocate bits by each signal and measure real PPL.
